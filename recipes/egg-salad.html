Egg Salad

Description

This healthy, delicious recipe is great to cool off in the hot summer! It makes for an appetizing breakfast or an afternoon snack. Enjoy it with crackers or between your favorite slices of toast.

Ingredients

  • 1/4 Cup Greek Yogurt
  • 1 Tb fresh lemon juice
  • 1 Tb chopped fresh dill
  • 1/4 ts salt
  • Pinch of black pepper
  • 4 Hard boiled eggs, chopped
  • Whole grain bread, crackers, or lettuce wraps
